What is Creative Labs?

Have you ever wanted to join a creative community at UCLA where you can collaborate with on real life projects, share skills, and just hangout?

Well, Creative Labs is going to be just that! Starting this quarter, we seek to bridge the gap between north and south campus at UCLA by bringing passionate and creative students together to work on cool projects.
